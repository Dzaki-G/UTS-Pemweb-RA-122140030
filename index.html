JOJI

Joji, whose real name is George Kusunoki Miller, is a Japanese-Australian singer, songwriter, and record producer known for his unique blend of genres, including R&B, lo-fi, and hip-hop. He was born on September 18, 1992, in Osaka, Japan, and moved to Australia at a young age. Joji first gained fame as a content creator on platforms like YouTube, where he created comedic and absurd videos under the name Filthy Frank and the music project Pink Guy.
